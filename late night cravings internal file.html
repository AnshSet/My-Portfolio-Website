Late Night Cravings

I recently created my first illustration, “Late Night Cravings”. This artwork was a journey of discovering my unique illustration style. Using vibrant and bold colors, I aimed to capture the emotions stirred by enjoying ramen late at night. I also experimented with playful and quirky fonts to enhance the visual impact.

Process Work

Step 1: Line Drawing

I started with simple line drawing iterations to refine the core composition of the illustration.

Step 2: Experimentation

In this step, I explored various elements, such as adding textures and vibrant colors.

Step 3: Background Textures

I experimented with various background textures, which was a lot of fun.
