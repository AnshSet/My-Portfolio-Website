About Inner Peace

This project helped me explore a wide range of textures, colors & different materials, like m-seal (epoxy compound), tissue paper, air dry clay, acrylic colors, and soft pastel.


The journey from concept to sculpting, detailing, painting, and presenting the artwork has been an enriching learning experience

What I Learnt

Exploring Diverse Materials

I learned how to work with various materials like epoxy compound, tissue paper, and air dry clay, gaining hands-on experience in manipulating them for creative projects.

Mastering Texture & Color Techniques

Experimenting with acrylic colors and soft pastels helped me understand how to blend and layer textures, colors, and finishes to create visually dynamic artwork.

Enhancing Crafting Skills

The project enhanced my ability to combine traditional and modern materials, pushing me to think outside the box and adapt my techniques to suit different textures and effects.
