About Cosmic Connect

'Cosmic Connect' is an interactive board game designed to foster deep and meaningful conversations. Through hand-illustrated activity cards and crystal tower pawns, players dive into various spiritual themes and explore thought-provoking questions with family and friends.

Features

Crystal Tower Pawns

Unique crystal tower pawns symbolize the spiritual journey each player embarks on.

Hand-Illustrated Cards

Each card is beautifully hand-drawn, offering an engaging and personal touch to the game.

Meaningful Gameplay

Fosters connection and deepens conversations about spirituality in a fun and engaging way.
